Agent Jett.
Han Sunwoo (Korean: 한선우) hails from South Korea, with possible links to the Insa-dong neighborhood in Seoul. As a chef in her early life, she was involved inan incident relating to her wind-like radiant powers at the restaurant she worked at, causing a "freak storm" that destroyed the building. Han fled the scene soon after. Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets he take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies before they even know what hit them.
